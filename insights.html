Confusing Power of Attorney Agreements? You're Not Alone.

Power of Attorney agreements in Indian real estate transactions are among the most commonly misunderstood instruments in civil law. When a PoA is drafted poorly — or executed without proper legal oversight — the consequences can be catastrophic: disputed title, fraudulent transfers, or the complete loss of a property purchased in good faith. This article explains why legal expertise is not optional when navigating PoA frameworks, and what can go wrong without it.
