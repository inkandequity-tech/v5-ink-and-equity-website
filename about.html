The Name

What Ink
& Equity
Means.

"Ink" represents the meticulous craft of legal writing — the careful construction of every petition, the precision of every argument filed. It signifies the weight of the written word in a court of law, and the tireless dedication to getting every word right.

"Equity" goes beyond the technical. It is the firm's core conviction — that justice is not a matter of resources or access, but a fundamental right owed to every person who walks through these doors. Whether the client is an individual fighting a service dispute or a corporate entity navigating a constitutional challenge, the standard of advocacy does not waver.

Together, the name is a promise: that every matter filed will bear the precision of excellent legal writing, and every outcome pursued will be measured by the standard of what is truly fair.

Ink & Equity is the natural evolution of Rishabh Raj & Associates — a practice that began in 2017 as a single advocate's conviction and grew, through the crucible of COVID-era courts, landmark High Court battles, and a rapidly expanding clientele, into the firm you see today. The legacy is intact. The ambition is larger.

The Journey

How We Got Here

2017

Seeds of Excellence — The First Brief

Fresh from Symbiosis Law School, Rishabh Raj secured a pre-placement offer at Knovos (formerly Capital Novus), a data intelligence firm in Gandhinagar, Gujarat — and was immediately deployed as the sole legal representative for the Delhi NCR region. Within weeks, he was working shoulder-to-shoulder with Tier I law firms, leading corporations, and Public Sector Undertakings.

↗ Pre-placement at Knovos · Delhi NCR Legal Representative
2018

Enrollment & Return to Roots

Enrolled with the Bar Council of Uttar Pradesh. Having absorbed the pace and complexity of corporate legal work in Delhi, Rishabh returned to Lucknow and joined the esteemed chambers of Shri Brijesh Kumar Saxena — a pioneering figure in civil law. Under his mentorship, Rishabh immersed himself in the civil courts, appearing regularly in property disputes, rent control matters, and succession proceedings.

↗ Bar Enrollment 2018 · Chambers of Shri Brijesh Kumar Saxena
2020

Undeterred Through the Pandemic

COVID-19 disrupted courts across India. Rishabh adapted early — mastering video conferencing appearances before courts, advocating through screens when courtrooms were closed, and maintaining the continuity his clients depended on. This resilience became the philosophical cornerstone of what Ink & Equity would eventually embody.

↗ Pioneered Virtual Court Appearances · Uninterrupted Client Advocacy
2021–22

The Birth of Rishabh Raj & Associates

With the unwavering guidance of his father — Shri Ram Raj Singh, a legal luminary of Uttar Pradesh — Rishabh founded Rishabh Raj & Associates. This was not a change of name; it was a declaration of independence and a commitment to a practice built on responsiveness, adaptability, and genuine client partnership. Assisting his father in complex matters opened the doors to constitutional law, service disputes, and intricate family litigation — the full breadth of the Indian legal landscape.

↗ Founding of Rishabh Raj & Associates · Constitutional & Service Law
2022–23

Championing Diverse Causes

The firm expanded its client roster significantly — representing prominent government bodies including the Sultanpur Zila Panchayat, Ghaziabad Development Authority, Deen Dayal Upadhyaya University, and the Meerut Development Authority. Simultaneously, Rishabh championed the cause of brick kilns, petrol pump operators, and individual property owners, cementing his reputation as an advocate of exceptional versatility.

↗ Government Bodies · Development Authorities · Real Estate Specialisation
2023–24

Vedant Joins — The Firm Becomes Independent

A pivotal turn arrived with Shri Vedant Bhargav joining the practice. His command over family law, criminal defence, and contract matters proved transformative — it was only after Vedant's arrival that the firm became truly autonomous. Together, Rishabh and Vedant built a practice capable of absorbing complex, multi-jurisdictional matters and delivering outcomes that spread by word of mouth through the Lucknow legal community.

↗ Vedant Bhargav Joins · Firm Achieves Full Independence
29.3.2024

Ink & Equity Is Born

On 29th March 2024, after years of deliberate evolution, the firm was reborn as Ink & Equity — a name that captures everything the practice stands for. Nine professionals. Twelve practice areas. Courts from Lucknow to New Delhi to the Supreme Court of India. The promise of seven years' experience, now carried forward with a sharper identity and a larger ambition.

The People

Leadership & Team

Nine professionals united by one conviction: that exceptional legal work requires both technical mastery and genuine human commitment.

Founding Partner

Rishabh Raj

BBA.LLB · Symbiosis Law School · Enrolled 2018

Rishabh Raj is the founding partner of Ink & Equity and the architect of everything the firm stands for. His legal journey began not in a courtroom, but in the boardrooms of Delhi NCR — as the sole legal representative for a data intelligence firm working with Tier I law firms and government undertakings. That exposure to the highest levels of corporate legal practice shaped a practitioner who understands both the letter and the spirit of the law.

Returning to Lucknow in 2018, Rishabh trained under Shri Brijesh Kumar Saxena — a master of civil law — before founding his own practice with the guidance of his father, Shri Ram Raj Singh. Over seven years, he has appeared before the Supreme Court of India, the Delhi High Court, the Allahabad High Court (both benches), and the civil and criminal courts of Uttar Pradesh. His practice spans constitutional law, real estate litigation, service matters, cyber crime, and writ jurisdiction. His ability to pivot, adapt, and advocate — demonstrated vividly during the pandemic courts — has become the firm's defining characteristic.

Constitutional Law Writ Jurisdiction Real Estate Service Matters Cyber Crime Supreme Court

Managing Partner

Vedant Bhargav

Managing Partner · Litigation, Family Law & Criminal Defence

Vedant Bhargav joined the firm at a pivotal moment in 2023 — and his arrival marked the point at which Rishabh Raj & Associates became truly independent. His expertise in family law, litigation strategy, criminal defence, and contract matters filled critical gaps in the firm's practice and gave it the depth to take on complex, multi-faceted matters across jurisdictions.

As Managing Partner, Vedant oversees the firm's day-to-day operations, client relationships, and case strategy. His command over family law — particularly in the emotionally charged terrain of custody, maintenance, and matrimonial disputes — has earned the firm a reputation as a counsel of both legal precision and genuine human sensitivity. His criminal practice spans bail applications, anticipatory bail, and appellate advocacy before the High Courts. Together with Rishabh, Vedant has built Ink & Equity into a practice where complex cases receive the strategic attention they deserve.

Litigation Strategy Family Law Criminal Defence Contract Law High Court Appeals

Partners

Gokel Seth

Junior Partner — Real Estate Law

Gokel Seth is the firm's dedicated specialist in real estate and property law — a practice area that demands not only legal acumen but an intimate understanding of the regulatory, municipal, and civil frameworks governing land and property in Uttar Pradesh and beyond. He brings deep expertise in RERA disputes, development authority matters, title verification, lease and rent litigation, and property title challenges before the civil courts and the High Courts of Allahabad and Delhi. His practice encompasses the full lifecycle of property disputes — from pre-litigation advisory and due diligence to complex multi-party litigation involving builders, development authorities, and government bodies. A calm and meticulous advocate, Gokel is known for his ability to untangle the most layered property disputes and present clear, decisive arguments in court. He has represented both individual property owners and institutional clients, including development authorities and private developers, before the courts of Uttar Pradesh and the Allahabad High Court.

Radhika Singh

Partner — Tax, Constitutional & Service Law

Radhika Singh is one of the firm's most formidable courtroom presences — a Partner whose practice spans three of the most intellectually demanding areas of Indian law: taxation, constitutional litigation, and service matters. In tax law, Radhika advises and represents clients on income tax disputes, GST challenges, assessment order challenges, and appellate proceedings before the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal and the High Courts. Her understanding of India's fiscal architecture — from direct tax to indirect tax — makes her an invaluable resource for both individual and institutional clients navigating the complexity of tax administration. In constitutional matters, she has appeared in fundamental rights challenges and public interest matters, bringing a rare combination of doctrinal depth and strategic clarity to the highest courts. Her service law practice spans promotion disputes, pension matters, suspension proceedings, and departmental enquiries — representing government servants before the Allahabad High Court with consistent success. Colleagues describe her as the firm's "constitutional conscience" — an advocate who understands that the law is never merely procedural.

Hanumant Lal Srivastava

Partner — Civil & Criminal Courts

Hanumant Lal Srivastava is the firm's anchor in the trial courts of Uttar Pradesh — a seasoned advocate with a commanding presence in both the Civil Courts and the Sessions Courts of the state. His civil practice spans property disputes, injunctions, partition suits, execution proceedings, and rent control matters — with a deep familiarity with the procedural landscape of UP's civil judiciary that only years of sustained courtroom presence can build. On the criminal side, Hanumant has an established and respected practice spanning bail applications, anticipatory bail, criminal trials, and appeals before the Sessions Courts and the High Court. His ability to navigate the pressures of criminal proceedings — with both composure and conviction — makes him an invaluable advocate for clients facing the most serious legal challenges. Within the firm, Hanumant is the bridge between the High Court practice of the partners and the trial court realities that form the foundation of most litigation — ensuring that cases are built from the ground up with the rigour they demand.

What We Stand For

Our Values

01

Precision Over Volume

Every matter we accept receives the full weight of our attention. We would rather argue ten cases well than fifty cases adequately. The quality of our work is not negotiable.

02

Equity as a Standard

We measure every outcome not just by legal victory but by genuine fairness. Our clients are not case numbers — their rights are real, and so is our responsibility to protect them.

03

Adaptability

We were practising before virtual courts before they became standard. We adapt to the law as it evolves, to technology as it changes practice, and to each client's unique circumstances.

04

Transparency

We speak plainly about the strengths and weaknesses of every matter. Our clients make decisions with full information — not false comfort. Trust is built on honesty, not on promises we cannot keep.

05

Legacy

We carry forward the traditions of a practice founded on discipline and mentorship — from Shri Ram Raj Singh's guidance to the chambers of Shri Brijesh Kumar Saxena. We know where we came from.

06

Accessibility

We don't charge for the first physical consultation. We respond to clients as real people with real problems — not as revenue units. Good legal counsel should not be a privilege of the wealthy alone.
