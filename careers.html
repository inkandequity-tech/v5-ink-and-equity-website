What We Offer

⚖️

Real Courtroom Exposure

From your first week, you will be in court. We don't warehouse junior advocates — we take them to hearings, give them responsibility, and build their confidence alongside their skills.

🏛️

Multi-Court Practice

Appear before the Supreme Court, Delhi HC, Allahabad HC, Lucknow Bench, and the civil courts of Uttar Pradesh. A breadth of exposure that most junior advocates spend years seeking.

🎓

Direct Mentorship

Work directly under Founding Partner Rishabh Raj and Managing Partner Vedant Bhargav — not buried under layers of seniority. Real mentorship, real feedback, real growth.

📚

Twelve Practice Areas

Specialise where your interests lie or develop breadth across our twelve practice areas. Constitutional law, real estate, cyber crime, tax, family law — the full range is available to you.

🤝

Equity, Not Just Work

We believe in building advocates, not just billing hours. Your career trajectory, your development, and your wellbeing matter to us as much as the matters we argue.

📍

Prime Location

Located at 1, Moti Mahal Marg, Hazratganj — moments from the Lucknow Bench of the Allahabad HC. At the centre of Lucknow's legal community.
